Tesla foundation

Tesla cars are made by Tesla Motors, an American company based in Palo Alto, California. Tesla’s Chief Executive Officer and chairman is the billionaire entrepreneur, Elon Musk, who cofounded PayPal and is the Chief Executive Officer of SpaceX, as of January 2015.

Tesla cars are manufactured at the Tesla factory in Fremont, California. Tesla originally purchased this site from Toyota in 2010. Currently, the Tesla factory employs around 3,000 people. Tesla Motors paid Toyota $47 million for the factory site. The location of the Tesla factory makes it unusual: it is one of the only electric cars built in the United States.

Types of Teslas

  • Tesla sports car
  • Tesla truck
  • Tesla Hatchback

Tesla sports car

The EV maker said at the time the Roadster prototype was able to sprint from zero to 60 mph (96 km/h) in 1.9 seconds – without specifying if this included a 1-ft rollout or not – and cover the quarter-mile in 8.8 seconds. The top speed would exceed 250 mph (402 km/h) while the range would reach 620 miles (998 kilometers).
